Turning Undelivered Mail Into Opportunity

We’re setting up vending machines to sell undelivered post — reducing waste, creating curiosity, and preventing items from being deleted forever.

About

Every year, thousands of parcels go unclaimed or undelivered. Instead of letting them go to waste, gives these forgotten items a second chance through our innovative vending machines. Each purchase is a mystery — what could be inside?

How It Works

1. We Collect

We partner with logistics and postal companies to acquire undelivered, unclaimed items that would otherwise be destroyed.

2. We Restock

Items are securely packed and loaded into our vending machines across various locations.

3. You Discover

Buy a mystery parcel from one of our vending machines — each purchase helps reduce waste and supports sustainable recycling.
